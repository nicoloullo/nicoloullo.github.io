Anchoring the Yield Curve Using Federal Funds Futures

Fed funds futures provide observable, market-based expectations of future short-term rates. We use them to anchor yield curve forecasts at the short end, where the Expectations Hypothesis holds. This forward-looking anchor is then propagated to the rest of the curve via a tilting procedure, improving on purely backward-looking term-structure models.
